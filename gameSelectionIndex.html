Game Instructions

Welcome to OL sudoku game! Your goal is to complete the puzzle.
You must have each one from the numbers 1-9 in each line, column and 3x3 block (marked on the board).
You can choose 1 of 3 regular difficulties:
  ● Easy (already 75% completed), 10 minutes to play.
  ● Medium (already 50% completed), 20 minutes to play.
  ● Hard (already 25% completed), 30 minutes to play.
In addition to that, you can play a custom game, where you can determine the percentage of board to complete and time to play!
Good luck and have fun! :D
